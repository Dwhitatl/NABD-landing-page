Mission & Purpose

A national voice for advocacy, education, and equal protection under the law.

NABD exists to advance civil and human rights, support public education, strengthen civic participation, promote environmental justice awareness, and help people navigate appropriate support pathways — with dignity and clarity.

We are a mission-driven organization committed to fair treatment, community empowerment, and principled public engagement for individuals and communities impacted by systemic inequity.

What your membership includes

Every NABD member — Youth and Adult — receives the full suite of benefits below, connecting you to advocacy, education, legal pathways, and a national community committed to equal justice.

⚖️
Legal Advocacy & Attorney Referral
Access NABD's network of civil rights and human rights attorneys. Members receive direct advocacy support and referral pathways to qualified legal counsel for matters brought before the organization.
🤝
Work Directly with Legal Counsel
Members have the opportunity to engage directly with legal professionals through NABD's referral network — ensuring you are connected to the right support for your specific situation.
📋
Case Management Team Review
All legal cases brought before NABD's Board of Directors receive a structured review by our dedicated case management team — ensuring every matter is evaluated with care, clarity, and purpose.
🏛
12 Human Rights Workshops Per Year
Attend up to 12 NABD-hosted human rights workshops annually — covering civil rights law, voting rights, environmental justice, community advocacy, and more.
🗳
Membership Voting Rights
Your voice matters. As an NABD member you hold voting rights on organizational matters, giving you a direct stake in shaping the future direction of the association.
🌐
State & National Conference Access
Members receive access to NABD's state and national conferences — premier gatherings of advocates, attorneys, and community leaders advancing equal justice across the country.
📖
NABD Quality Magazine
Receive NABD's member publication featuring in-depth coverage of civil rights developments, legal advocacy news, community spotlights, and organizational updates.
🎙
Monthly Podcast — American Injustice
Exclusive member access to NABD's monthly podcast series examining systemic injustice in America — featuring legal experts, advocates, and community voices on the front lines of change.
All benefits apply to both Youth ($75/yr) and Adult ($175/yr) membership tiers. Annual membership renews automatically via Stripe.
